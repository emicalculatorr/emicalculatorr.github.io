EMI Calculator

What is EMI?

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay a loan. An EMI includes both the principal amount and the interest component.

How EMI is Calculated

The EMI is calculated using the following formula:

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N − 1]

  • P = Loan amount (Principal)
  • R = Monthly interest rate (Annual rate / 12 / 100)
  • N = Loan tenure in months

Benefits of Using an EMI Calculator

  • Gives instant and accurate results
  • Allows comparison between different loan options
  • Saves time and avoids manual calculations
